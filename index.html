Some Amazing Geniuses

Albert Einstein was one of the greatest physicists of all time.

Serbian-American engineer and physicist Nikola Tesla (1856-1943) made dozens of breakthroughs in the production, transmission and application of electric power.

While Leonardo da Vinci is best known as an artist, his work as a scientist and an inventor make him a true Renaissance man.

Steve Jobs was a charismatic pioneer of the personal computer era. With Steve Wozniak, Jobs founded Apple Inc. in 1976 and transformed the company into a world leader in telecommunications.
